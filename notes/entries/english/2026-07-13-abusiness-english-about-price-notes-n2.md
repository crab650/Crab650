# Talking About Prices

## Price goes up

> The price of the product has risen.

產品價格上漲了。

---

## Price goes down

> The price of the product has fallen.

產品價格下降了。

---

## Price stays the same

> The price of the product is steady.

產品價格保持穩定。

---

## Using plural

> Product prices have risen.

產品價格上漲了。

> Product prices have fallen.

產品價格下降了。

> Product prices are steady.

產品價格保持穩定。

---

# Important Grammar

## rise vs raise

### rise（自己上升）

- Prices rise.
- Sales rise.
- Demand rises.

---

### raise（提高某樣東西）

- Raise prices.
- Raise salaries.
- Raise taxes.

✅ We raise our prices.

❌ Prices raise.

---

# Present Perfect

Today we learned many Present Perfect sentences.

Examples

- Prices have risen.
- Prices have fallen.
- Sales have increased.
- Costs have risen.

Meaning:

The action happened in the past and still affects the present.

# Talking About Markets

## Positive market

- Prices are high.
- People are buying.
- Investors are optimistic.

Example

> The tone of the market is positive.

市場氣氛樂觀。

---

## Negative market

- Prices fall.
- People stop buying.
- Companies become careful.

Example

> The market mood is negative.

市場情緒悲觀。

---

# Tone vs Mood

| Tone | Mood |
|------|------|
| 基調 | 情緒 |
| More objective | More emotional |
| Market tone | Market mood |

Examples

The tone of the market is positive.

The market mood is positive.

# Today's Sentence Patterns

## Pattern 1

The price of + noun + has risen.

Example

> The price of steel has risen.

---

## Pattern 2

The price of + noun + has fallen.

Example

> The price of oil has fallen.

---

## Pattern 3

The price of + noun + is steady.

Example

> The price of cotton is steady.

---

## Pattern 4

Rising + noun + prices have affected + industry.

Example

> Rising oil prices have affected the shipping industry.

---

## Pattern 5

There is a crisis in the ______ industry.

Example

> There is a crisis in the automobile industry.

---

## Pattern 6

The ______ industry is facing a crisis.

Example

> The textile industry is facing a crisis.

---

## Pattern 7

The tone of the market is positive.

Example

> The tone of the semiconductor market is positive.
